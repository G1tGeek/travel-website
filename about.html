About Hitch Hickers

Founded with a passion to uncover the unexplored, Hitch Hickers is more than a travel company—it's a dream project by a bunch of wanderers who believe that every Indian deserves to witness the magic of their homeland. Whether it's the snowy peaks of Himachal or the beaches of Andaman, we craft journeys that turn your travel dreams into lifetime memories.

“Every step you take with us leads to a story worth telling.”

With a focus on affordability, authenticity, and adventure, we ensure that each trip is not just a vacation but an experience. Our curated packages, local tie-ups, and on-ground teams ensure comfort and thrill in equal measure.
